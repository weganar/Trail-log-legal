Traillog Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-16

Traillog is a location timeline app. This policy explains what data the app uses and how you control it.

Data We Process

Depending on the features you use, Traillog may process:

  • Location data (to build your timeline).
  • Motion and fitness data (for steps and movement enrichment).
  • Health data (for heart rate and workout metrics, when granted).
  • Photo metadata and selected photo references (for timeline photo matching, when granted).
  • Optional notes, tags, and titles you add to segments.
  • iCloud sharing metadata for people you share with.

Where Data Is Stored

  • Data is stored locally on your device.
  • If iCloud sync/sharing is enabled, data is synced using Apple's CloudKit infrastructure tied to your Apple ID.
  • We do not run our own location-data backend outside Apple services for core timeline storage.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to:

  • Build and display your timeline (stays, trips, activities, lifestyle sessions).
  • Provide statistics, summaries, and map views.
  • Support optional sharing and collaboration features.
  • Support optional export features.

Sharing

  • Sharing is optional.
  • You control when and with whom data is shared.
  • You can revoke access by removing participants or deleting shares.

Permissions

Traillog requests only the permissions needed for enabled features:

  • Location
  • Motion and Fitness
  • Health
  • Photos

You can revoke permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Data Retention and Control

You can:

  • Stop tracking at any time.
  • Configure location retention duration.
  • Export your data (where available).
  • Remove shared access by deleting shares.

Third-Party Services

Traillog relies on Apple frameworks and services such as CloudKit, MapKit, WeatherKit, HealthKit, and Photos APIs as part of app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Traillog is not intended for children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at the Privacy Policy URL provided in the app.
