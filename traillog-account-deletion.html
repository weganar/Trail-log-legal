Traillog Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026-04-29 · Published by Weganar

This page explains how to delete your Traillog account and what happens to your data.

Option 1: Delete from inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open Traillog on your Android device.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm when prompted.

Your Firebase account, your cloud-synced trip data in Firestore, and any active shares are removed within a few seconds of confirmation. Local data on the device is cleared when you uninstall the app.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you no longer have the app installed, or you cannot sign in, you can request deletion by email.

Send an email to support@weganar.com from the address associated with your Traillog account, with the subject:

Traillog account deletion request

We will process the request and confirm completion by email within 30 days. Most requests are completed within 72 hours.

What gets deleted

DataDeleted on request?Retention
Trip recordings, timeline segments, personal records Yes Removed from Firestore immediately; on-device copy cleared on uninstall.
Shared trips you created Yes Recipients lose access when the share is deleted.
Firebase Authentication account Yes Google / Apple / anonymous account is deleted.
Cloud-synced settings and preferences Yes Removed with the account.
Crash reports (Firebase Crashlytics) Partially Anonymous crash entries are retained up to 90 days for product stability analysis. No raw user identifiers are stored — IDs are redacted before upload.
Anonymous analytics (Firebase Analytics) Partially Aggregated, non-identifying analytics are retained up to 14 months per Google's default retention. No personally identifiable information is linked to this data.

Children

Traillog is not directed at children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has created an account, email support@weganar.com and we will delete the account.
