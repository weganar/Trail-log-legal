What to Include

To help us investigate, include:

  • Traillog version and build number from the app or TestFlight.
  • Your iPhone, iPad, or Apple Watch model and OS version.
  • The steps that led to the issue and what you expected to happen.
  • Whether the issue involves iCloud sharing, Apple Watch, permissions, or a subscription.

Do not send precise location history, health details, or other sensitive information unless it is necessary and you choose to do so.
